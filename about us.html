Brief Summary

Brief Description:


Prayagraj Power Generation Company Limited (PPGCL) has set up a 3 x 660 MW coal fired Thermal Power Plant at Tehsil Bara, district Allahabad, Uttar Pradesh, India. The Power Plant consists of three (3) nos. of Turbine Generator sets of 660 MW each and three (3) nos. of pulverized coal fired supercritical Steam Generators (Boilers) and the Balance of Plant.

All the three Units have been commissioned and achieved COD of the Units as below:

  • Unit-1: 29th February 2016
  • Unit-2: 10th September 2016
  • Unit-3: 26th May 2017

Plant Technology:


The Units are designed for Coal based Super-critical Once Through Sliding Pressure technology. The BTG package has been procured from BHEL.

Power Evacuation Arrangement:

Power evacuation is done at 765 kV Single Circuit Transmission Line, which is connected to Manipuri Substation of UPPTCL. In addition to that, one (1) No. of 400 kV Double Circuit line is also provided to evacuate power. This line is connected withRewa Road sub-station.


The second circuit of 765 kV line commissioning is in Progress. Erection of almost all towers has been completed, but stringing work is pending. Work is in the scope of UPPTCL.

Power Selling Arrangement:

PPGCL has entered into a 25 years Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with the 5 distribution utilities (Procurers) of the state of Uttar Pradesh. 90% of the power generated from project(netted of for auxiliary power consumption 7.5%) is to be supplied to the five Procurers under this PPA. The salient features of PPA are attached as ANNEXURE - 12 to this report. For balance capacity, currently there is no short term PPA in existence though it can be sold through merchant basis and bilateral arrangements.

Water Supply Arrangement:

Water required for the project is drawn from Yamuna River, which is at a distance of 17 km from the plant site. River Water Intake Pump House is installed at the bank of the river and necessary power is supplied from the power station itself through a 33 kV Over Headline, however it is a single circuit line and hence there is no redundancy of power supply.
